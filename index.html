Our Origins

Founded in 1993 in the Philippines, Swiftlink began as a small, local delivery service with a mission to provide fast, reliable shipping for customers. From our humble beginnings, we recognized the growing need for efficient logistics solutions, which spurred our expansion into the global market. Over the years, we’ve steadily built a robust infrastructure and an ever-expanding network of partners, agents, and locations across more than 30 countries. This expansion has enabled us to meet the diverse logistics needs of businesses worldwide, while always staying true to our commitment to service excellence.

Today, Swiftlink is a global leader in logistics and transportation, offering services for businesses of all sizes. Despite our rapid growth, we continue to focus on the personalized service that made us successful in the beginning. We are dedicated to providing reliable and efficient solutions that meet the evolving needs of our clients.

Our Services

At Swiftlink, we understand that no two businesses are alike, which is why we offer a wide range of logistics services designed to meet the unique needs of our customers. Our services are powered by advanced technology and innovative strategies to ensure efficiency and reliability. Secure Non Vessel Operating Common Carrier (NVOCC)

Shipping doesn’t have to be complicated or costly. Our NVOCC services provide a secure, flexible way to move cargo without requiring a full container. With reliable freight forwarding and a strong logistics network, we ensure smooth import and export, both locally and globally.

No need to wait for a full load—ship your goods as soon as they’re ready. Plus, our NVOCC brokers help stabilize LCL shipping costs, ensuring you get competitive rates for efficient, cost-effective transport.

Cross Docking Logistics Solutions

Keep inventory moving and cut costs with our cross-docking freight services. By sorting and reassembling cargo for quick transfer, we minimize storage time and speed up deliveries. Your shipments move seamlessly from inbound to outbound transport, reducing warehouse reliance and handling.

With a well-planned logistics process, we help businesses save time and money by eliminating unnecessary storage. Our efficient cross-docking operations simplify supply chains, lower inventory costs, and ensure goods reach their destination faster.

Specialized Logistics

We offer reverse logistics for hassle-free returns, document return services for completed paperwork, and on-site management for secure handling. Our cash-on-delivery option ensures convenient payments with fast remittance to your account, streamlining transactions for both you and your customers.

From parcels to large-scale cargo, we provide cost-effective logistics solutions that keep your supply chain running smoothly. With efficient handling and timely deliveries, we help businesses stay on track and meet customer demands.
